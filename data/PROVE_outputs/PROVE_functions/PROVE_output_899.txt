- Provide multiple IR and VIS images of a primary target during a single primary observation pass
- Provide IR images of secondary targets during a single secondary observation pass
- Provide multiple VIS images of a tertiary target during a single tertiary observation pass
- Distribute images over the observation window for a range of viewing angles in primary and tertiary observation passes
- Perform primary target observations for a lifetime of at least 1 month, with a goal of 12 months
- Automate observations based on a pre-determined schedule with the ability to prioritize scenarios
- Ensure a minimum elevation angle of 30 degrees for target observations
- Provide narrow-angle IR and VIS images for science observations
- Maintain imager field of views alignment within 12 degrees
- Provide IR images with a spatial sampling distance (SSD) at nadir of 300m (threshold) and 90m (goal)
- Provide narrow-angle VIS images with a SSD at nadir of 50m (threshold) and 15m (goal)
- Provide a minimum of 10 science images per primary or tertiary observation pass, with a goal of 50
- Provide a minimum of 1 science image per target per secondary observation pass
- Ensure a temporal resolution for primary target observations of a minimum of 16 days, with a goal of 5 days
- Maintain the NedT of the IR camera at a minimum of 0.5K and a goal of 0.05K, with uncooled operation
- Perform radiometric calibration of the IR camera at least once per target, ideally before capture begins
- Provide geolocation accuracy of the target within one SSD over land
- Achieve a pointing accuracy of 1 degree
- Downlink image data within 1 month (threshold) and 7 days (goal) of an observation pass
- Size onboard storage for at least one primary target image set, with a goal of 10 sets
- Transfer data to the bus for downlink or additional storage
- Ensure total payload mass does not exceed 2.66kg
- Ensure total payload power usage does not exceed 14W
- Ensure total payload volume does not exceed 2U
- Include imaging, standby, safe, and other TBD non-nominal modes as needed
- Qualify mission hardware to GSFC-STD-7000B levels and durations
