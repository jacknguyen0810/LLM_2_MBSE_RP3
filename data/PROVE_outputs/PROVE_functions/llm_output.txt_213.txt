- Provide multiple infrared (IR) and visible spectrum (VIS) images of a primary target during a single primary observation pass
- Provide IR images of secondary targets during a single secondary observation pass
- Provide multiple VIS images of a tertiary target during a single tertiary observation pass
- Distribute images over the observation window to provide a range of viewing angles for primary and tertiary targets
- Perform primary target observations for a minimum of 1 month, with a goal of 12 months
- Automate observations based on a pre-determined schedule and allow uplink of priority scenarios
- Maintain a minimum elevation angle of 30 degrees for target observations
- Provide narrow-angle IR and VIS images for scientific observations
- Ensure a minimum swath width of 80km at nadir for scientific images
- Maintain alignment of imager field of views to within 12 degrees
- Provide IR images with a spatial sampling distance (SSD) at nadir of 300m (threshold), with a goal of 90m
- Provide narrow-angle VIS images with a SSD at nadir of 50m (threshold), with a goal of 15m
- Capture a minimum of 10 science images per primary or tertiary observation pass
- Capture a minimum of 1 science image per target during a secondary observation pass
- Achieve a temporal resolution of 16 days for primary target observations, with a goal of 5 days
- Ensure a minimum NedT of the IR camera of 0.5K, with a goal of 0.05K
- Perform radiometric calibration of the IR camera at least once per target capture
- Provide geolocation accuracy of one SSD over land
- Achieve a pointing accuracy of 1 degree
- Downlink image data within 1 month threshold, with a goal of 7 days
- Size onboard storage for at least one primary target image set, with a goal of 10 sets
- Transfer data to the bus for downlink or additional storage
- Limit total payload mass to not exceed 2.66kg
- Limit total payload power usage to not exceed 14W
- Limit total payload volume to not exceed 2U
- Include imaging, standby, safe, and other TBD non-nominal modes
- Qualify mission hardware to GSFC-STD-7000B levels and durations
