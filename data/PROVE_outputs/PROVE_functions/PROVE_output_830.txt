- Provide multiple IR and VIS images of a given primary target during a single primary observation pass.
- Provide IR images of given secondary targets during a single secondary observation pass.
- Provide multiple VIS images of a given tertiary target during a single tertiary observation pass.
- Distribute images over the observation window in MIS-001 and MIS-003 to provide a range of viewing angles.
- Perform automated observations based on a pre-determined schedule of standard targets and ability to uplink priority observation scenarios.
- Maintain a minimum elevation angle of 30 degrees for target observations.
- Provide narrow-angle IR and VIS images for science observations.
- Ensure a minimum swath width of 80km at nadir for scientific images.
- Maintain an alignment of imager field of views to within 12 degrees in the payload structure.
- Provide IR images with a spatial sampling distance of 300m at nadir, with a goal of 90m.
- Provide narrow-angle VIS images with a spatial sampling distance of 50m at nadir, with a goal of 15m.
- Provide a minimum of 10 science images per primary or tertiary observation pass, with a goal of 50.
- Provide a minimum of 1 science image per target per secondary observation pass.
- Ensure a temporal resolution for primary target observations of a minimum of 16 days, with a goal of 5 days.
- Achieve a NedT of the IR camera of a minimum of 0.5K, with a goal of 0.05K, using an uncooled IR camera.
- Perform radiometric calibration of the IR camera a minimum of once per target, ideally before target capture begins.
- Provide geolocation of the target to within one spatial sampling distance over land.
- Achieve a pointing accuracy of 1 degree.
- Downlink image data from an observation pass within a threshold of 1 month, with a goal of 7 days.
- Size onboard storage for one primary target image set, with a goal of 10 sets.
- Transfer data to the bus for downlink or additional storage.
- Ensure the total payload mass does not exceed 2.66kg.
- Ensure the total payload power usage does not exceed 14W.
- Ensure the total payload volume does not exceed 2U.
- Include imaging, standby, safe, and other TBD non-nominal modes in the mission operations.
- Qualify mission hardware to the levels and durations specified in GSFC-STD-7000B.
