- Perform observations based on a pre-determined schedule with the ability to prioritize scenarios

- Provide geolocation accuracy of the target within one SSD over land
- Achieve a pointing accuracy of 1 degree

- Store imaging data, position data, and health telemetry appropriately
- Operate in different modes and accommodate various imaging sequences
- Operate in a temperature range of -30 to +50 degrees C under vacuum conditions
