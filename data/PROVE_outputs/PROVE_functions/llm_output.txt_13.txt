- Acquire multiple IR and VIS images of a primary target during a single primary observation pass
- Capture IR images of secondary targets during a single secondary observation pass
- Obtain multiple VIS images of a tertiary target during a single tertiary observation pass
- Distribute images over the observation window to provide various viewing angles
- Perform primary target observations for at least 1 month, with a goal of 12 months
- Automate observations based on a pre-determined schedule, with the ability to prioritize certain scenarios
- Maintain a minimum observation elevation angle of 30 degrees
- Provide narrow-angle IR and VIS images for scientific observations
- Ensure minimum swath width of 80km at nadir for scientific images
- Maintain alignment of imager field of views within 12 degrees
- Achieve spatial sampling distance (SSD) for IR images at nadir of 300m (threshold) with a goal of 90m
- Achieve SSD for narrow-angle VIS images at nadir of 50m (threshold) with a goal of 15m
- Capture a minimum of 10 science images per primary or tertiary observation pass, with a goal of 50
- Provide at least 1 science image per target during a secondary observation pass
- Ensure temporal resolution for primary target observations of a minimum 16 days, with a goal of 5 days
- Maintain NedT of the uncooled IR camera at a minimum of 0.5K, with a goal of 0.05K
- Perform radiometric calibration of the IR camera at least once per target, ideally before target capture begins
- Provide geolocation of the target within one SSD over land
- Achieve pointing accuracy of 1 degree
- Downlink image data from an observation pass within a threshold of 1 month, with a goal of 7 days
- Design onboard storage to accommodate one primary target image set, with a goal of 10 sets
- Transfer data to the bus for downlink or additional storage
- Ensure total payload mass does not exceed 2.66kg
- Ensure total payload power usage does not exceed 14W
- Ensure total payload volume does not exceed 2U
- Include imaging, standby, safe, and other TBD non-nominal modes in the mission
- Qualify mission hardware to the standards specified in GSFC-STD-7000B
