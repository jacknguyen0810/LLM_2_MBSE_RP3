- Provide multiple IR and VIS images of a given primary target during a single primary observation pass
- Provide IR images of given secondary targets during a single secondary observation pass
- Provide multiple VIS images of a given tertiary target during a single tertiary observation pass
- Distribute images over the observation window to provide a range of viewing angles for primary and tertiary targets
- Perform observations automatically based on pre-determined schedule, with ability to uplink priority scenarios
- Maintain a minimum elevation angle of 30 degrees for target observations
- Provide narrow-angle IR and VIS images for scientific observations
- Ensure a minimum swath width of 80km at nadir for scientific images
- Maintain alignment of imager field of views to within 12 degrees
- Provide IR images with spatial sampling distance (SSD) at nadir of 300m (threshold), goal of 90m
- Provide narrow-angle VIS images with SSD at nadir of 50m (threshold), goal of 15m
- Capture a minimum of 10 science images per primary or tertiary observation pass
- Capture a minimum of 1 science image per target per secondary observation pass
- Achieve a temporal resolution of minimum 16 days, goal of 5 days, for primary target observations
- Ensure the IR camera has a NedT of minimum 0.5K, goal of 0.05K, and is uncooled
- Perform radiometric calibration of the IR camera a minimum of once per target, ideally before capture
- Provide geolocation of the target to within one SSD over land
- Achieve a pointing accuracy of 1 degree
- Downlink image data within a threshold of 1 month, goal of 7 days
- Have onboard storage sized for one primary target image set, goal of 10 sets
- Transfer data to the bus for downlink or additional storage
- Ensure total payload mass does not exceed 2.66kg
- Ensure total payload power usage does not exceed 14W
- Ensure total payload volume does not exceed 2U
- Include modes for imaging, standby, and safe, with other non-nominal modes as needed
- Qualify mission hardware to the levels specified in GSFC-STD-7000B
