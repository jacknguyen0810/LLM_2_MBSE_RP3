1. **Primary Observation Pass Functions:**
   - Capture and provide multiple Infrared (IR) and Visible (VIS) images of the primary target.
   - Distribute IR and VIS images over the observation window to achieve a range of viewing angles.
   - Achieve a temporal resolution for primary target observations of a minimum of 16 days, with a goal of 5 days.

2. **Secondary Observation Pass Functions:**
   - Provide IR images of given secondary targets during a single secondary observation pass.
   - Ensure a minimum of 1 science image per target per secondary observation pass.

3. **Tertiary Observation Pass Functions:**
   - Provide multiple VIS images of a given tertiary target during a single tertiary observation pass.
   - Achieve a minimum swath width of 80km at nadir for scientific images.

4. **Automated Observation Functions:**
   - Implement automated observations based on a predetermined schedule for standard targets.
   - Allow the uplinking of priority observation scenarios.
