- Provide multiple IR and VIS images of a given primary target during a single primary observation pass
- Provide IR images of given secondary targets during a single secondary observation pass
- Provide multiple VIS images of a given tertiary target during a single tertiary observation pass
- Distribute images over the observation window for a range of viewing angles
- Perform observations based on pre-determined schedule with ability to uplink priority scenarios
- Maintain a minimum elevation angle of 30 degrees for target observations
- Provide narrow-angle IR and VIS images for science observations
- Ensure minimum swath width of 80km at nadir for scientific images
- Maintain alignment of imager field of views within 12 degrees
- Provide IR images with spatial sampling distance of 300m (threshold) and 90m (goal) at nadir
- Provide narrow-angle VIS images with spatial sampling distance of 50m (threshold) and 15m (goal) at nadir
- Provide a minimum of 10 science images per primary or tertiary observation pass
- Provide a minimum of 1 science image per target per secondary observation pass
- Achieve temporal resolution of 16 days (minimum) and 5 days (goal) for primary target observations
- Ensure NedT of IR camera is at least 0.5K (threshold) and 0.05K (goal)
- Perform radiometric calibration of IR camera at least once per target, ideally before capture
- Provide geolocation of target to within one spatial sampling distance over land
- Achieve pointing accuracy of 1 degree
- Downlink image data within 1 month (threshold) and 7 days (goal) of observation pass
- Size onboard storage for at least one primary target image set (MIS-012), with a goal of 10 sets
- Transfer data to the bus for downlink or additional storage
- Ensure total payload mass does not exceed 2.66kg
- Ensure total payload power usage does not exceed 14W
- Ensure total payload volume does not exceed 2U
- Support different operational modes including imaging, standby, safe, and other TBD non-nominal modes
- Qualify mission hardware to levels and durations specified in GSFC-STD-7000B
