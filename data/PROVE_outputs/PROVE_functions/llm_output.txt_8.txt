- Provide multiple IR and VIS images of a given primary target during a single primary observation pass
- Provide IR images of given secondary targets during a single secondary observation pass
- Provide multiple VIS images of a given tertiary target during a single tertiary observation pass
- Distribute images provided in primary and tertiary observation passes over the observation window to provide a range of viewing angles
- Perform observations based on a pre-determined schedule and ability to uplink priority observation scenarios
- Maintain minimum elevation angle of 30 degrees for observations
- Provide narrow-angle IR and VIS images for science observations
- Ensure minimum swath width of 80km at nadir for scientific images
- Maintain alignment of imager field of views to within 12 degrees
- Provide IR images with spatial sampling distance (SSD) at nadir of 300m (threshold) and goal of 90m
- Provide narrow-angle VIS images with SSD at nadir of 50m (threshold) and goal of 15m
- Provide a minimum of 10 science images, with a goal of 50, per primary or tertiary observation pass
- Provide a minimum of 1 science image per target per secondary observation pass
- Maintain a temporal resolution for primary target observations at a minimum of 16 days, with a goal of 5 days
- Ensure NedT of the IR camera is a minimum of 0.5K with a goal of 0.05K
- Perform radiometric calibration of the IR camera a minimum of once per target, ideally before the target capture begins
- Provide geolocation of the target to within one SSD over land
- Achieve pointing accuracy of 1 degree
- Downlink image data within a threshold of 1 month, with a goal of 7 days
- Ensure onboard storage is sized for one primary target image set, with a goal of 10 primary target image sets
- Transfer data to the bus for downlink or additional storage
- Limit total payload mass to not exceed 2.66kg
- Ensure total payload power usage does not exceed 14W
- Keep total payload volume within 2U
- Provide imaging, standby, safe, and other TBD non-nominal modes as needed
- Qualify mission hardware to the levels and durations specified in GSFC-STD-7000B.
