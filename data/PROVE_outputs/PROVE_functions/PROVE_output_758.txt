- The system shall provide multiple IR and VIS images of a primary target during a single observation pass.
- The system shall provide IR images of secondary targets during a single observation pass.
- The system shall provide multiple VIS images of a tertiary target during a single observation pass.
- The images provided for primary and tertiary targets shall be distributed over the observation window to provide a range of viewing angles.
- The system shall perform observations based on a pre-determined schedule of standard targets, with the ability to uplink priority scenarios.
- Observations of targets shall have a minimum elevation angle of 30 degrees.
- The system shall provide narrow-angle IR and VIS images for scientific observations.
- Scientific images shall have a minimum swath width of 80km at nadir.
- The payload structure shall maintain the alignment of imager field of views within 12 degrees.
- IR images shall have a spatial sampling distance (SSD) at nadir of 300m (threshold), with a goal of 90m.
- VIS images shall have a SSD at nadir of 50m (threshold), with a goal of 15m.
- A minimum of 10 science images, with a goal of 50, shall be provided per primary or tertiary observation pass.
- A minimum of 1 science image per target shall be provided per secondary observation pass.
- The system shall have a temporal resolution for primary target observations of a minimum of 16 days, with a goal of 5 days.
- The IR camera's NedT shall be a minimum of 0.5K, with a goal of 0.05K, and shall be uncooled.
- Radiometric calibration of the IR camera shall be performed at least once per target, ideally before target capture begins.
- The system shall provide geolocation of the target to within one SSD over land.
- The system shall achieve a pointing accuracy of 1 degree.
- Image data from an observation pass shall be downlinked within a threshold of 1 month, with a goal of 7 days.
- Onboard storage shall be sized for at least one primary target image set, with a goal of 10 primary target image sets.
- Data shall be transferred to the bus for downlink or additional storage.
- The total payload mass shall not exceed 2.66kg.
- The total payload power usage shall not exceed 14W.
- The total payload volume shall not exceed 2U.
- The mission shall operate in imaging, standby, safe, and other TBD non-nominal modes as required.
- The hardware shall be qualified to the levels and durations specified in GSFC-STD-7000B.
