- Provide multiple IR and VIS images of a primary target in a single observation pass
- Provide IR images of secondary targets in a single observation pass
- Provide multiple VIS images of a tertiary target in a single observation pass
- Distribute images over the observation window for a range of viewing angles
- Automate observations based on a predetermined schedule with priority uplink capability
- Maintain a minimum elevation angle of 30 degrees for target observations
- Provide narrow-angle IR and VIS images for science observations
- Maintain alignment of imager field of views within 12 degrees
- Provide IR images with spatial sampling distance (SSD) at nadir of 300m (threshold), 90m (goal)
- Provide narrow-angle VIS images with SSD at nadir of 50m (threshold), 15m (goal)
- Provide a minimum of 10 science images per primary or tertiary observation pass
- Provide a minimum of 1 science image per target per secondary observation pass
- Perform primary target observations every 16 days (minimum), every 5 days (goal)
- Maintain IR camera NedT of 0.5K (minimum), 0.05K (goal); uncooled camera
- Perform radiometric calibration of IR camera at least once per target
- Provide geolocation of target within one SSD over land
- Achieve pointing accuracy of 1 degree
- Downlink image data within 1 month (threshold), 7 days (goal)
- Ensure onboard storage for one primary target image set (MIS-012), 10 sets (goal)
- Transfer data to the bus for downlink or additional storage
- Limit total payload mass to 2.66kg
- Limit total payload power usage to 14W
- Limit total payload volume to 2U
- Support system modes: imaging, standby, safe, and other TBD non-nominal modes as needed
- Qualify mission hardware to GSFC-STD-7000B levels and durations
- Meet electrical requirements for power supply, monitoring, and component connections
- Interface CDH subsystem with IR and VIS cameras, payload computer, and bus
- Ensure CDH system capabilities for imaging, health monitoring, memory, operations, temperature, and environmental factors
- Implement structural requirements for payload structure to support components and fit within specified volume
- Ensure thermal requirements compliance for all payload components regarding operating and storage temperatures.
