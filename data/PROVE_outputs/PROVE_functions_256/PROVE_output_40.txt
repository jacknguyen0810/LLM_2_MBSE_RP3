- Capture multiple IR and VIS images of a primary target during a single primary observation pass.
- Capture IR images of secondary targets during a single secondary observation pass.
- Capture multiple VIS images of a tertiary target during a single tertiary observation pass.
- Distribute images over the observation window to provide various viewing angles for primary and tertiary targets.
- Perform primary target observations for a minimum of 1 month with a goal of 12 months.
- Automate observations based on a predetermined schedule with the ability to prioritize scenarios.
- Maintain a minimum elevation angle of 30 degrees for target observations.
- Provide narrow-angle IR and VIS images for scientific observations.
- Ensure a minimum swath width of 80km at nadir for scientific images.
- Maintain alignment of imager field of views within 12 degrees.
- Achieve a spatial sampling distance (SSD) of 300m at nadir for IR images with a goal of 90m.
- Achieve a SSD of 50m at nadir for VIS images with a goal of 15m.
- Provide a minimum of 10 science images per primary or tertiary observation pass with a goal of 50.
- Provide a minimum of 1 science image per target per secondary observation pass.
- Maintain a temporal resolution of 16 days for primary target observations with a goal of 5 days.
- Ensure the NedT of the IR camera is a minimum of 0.5K with a goal of 0.05K, and ensure it is uncooled.
- Perform radiometric calibration of the IR camera at least once per target, ideally before target capture begins.
- Provide geolocation of the target within one SSD over land.
- Achieve pointing accuracy of 1 degree.
- Downlink image data within 1 month with a goal of 7 days.
- Size onboard storage for at least one primary target image set with a goal of 10 sets.
- Transfer data to the bus for downlink or additional storage.
- Ensure the total payload mass does not exceed 2.66kg.
- Ensure the total payload power usage does not exceed 14W.
- Ensure the total payload volume does not exceed 2U.
- Implement operational modes including imaging, standby, safe, and other non-nominal modes as needed.
- Qualify mission hardware to the levels and durations specified in GSFC-STD-7000B.
