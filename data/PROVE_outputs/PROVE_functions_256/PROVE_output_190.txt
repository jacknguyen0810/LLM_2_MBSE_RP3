- Provide multiple IR and VIS images of a primary target during a single primary observation pass
- Provide IR images of secondary targets during a single secondary observation pass
- Provide multiple VIS images of a tertiary target during a single tertiary observation pass
- Distribute images over the observation window to provide a range of viewing angles
- Perform primary target observations for a lifetime of 1 month (threshold) and 12 months (goal)
- Automate observations based on a pre-determined schedule with the ability to uplink priority scenarios
- Maintain a minimum elevation angle of 30 degrees for target observations
- Provide narrow-angle IR and VIS images for science observations
- Ensure a minimum swath width of 80km at nadir for scientific images
- Maintain alignment of the imager field of views within 12 degrees
- Provide IR images with a spatial sampling distance (SSD) at nadir of 300m (threshold) and 90m (goal)
- Provide narrow-angle VIS images with a SSD at nadir of 50m (threshold) and 15m (goal)
- Provide a minimum of 10 science images (goal of 50) per primary or tertiary observation pass
- Provide a minimum of 1 science image per target per secondary observation pass
- Achieve a temporal resolution of 16 days (threshold) and 5 days (goal) for primary target observations
- Ensure the IR camera has a minimum NedT of 0.5K (goal of 0.05K) and is uncooled
- Perform radiometric calibration of the IR camera once per target, ideally before capture
- Provide geolocation of the target within one SSD over land
- Achieve a pointing accuracy of 1 degree
- Downlink image data within 1 month (threshold) and 7 days (goal) of an observation pass
- Size onboard storage for one primary target image set (threshold) and 10 sets (goal)
- Transfer data to the bus for downlink or additional storage
- Ensure total payload mass does not exceed 2.66kg
- Ensure total payload power usage does not exceed 14W
- Ensure total payload volume does not exceed 2U
- Implement operational modes including imaging, standby, safe, and other TBD non-nominal modes
- Qualify mission hardware to the levels and durations in GSFC-STD-7000B
