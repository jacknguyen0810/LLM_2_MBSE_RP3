- Pre Launch Phase (manufacture, assembly, testing, launch-vehicle integration, and launch preparation)
- Launch Phase (the payload is launched into orbit)
- Deployment and Trajectory Correction Phase (Observatory separates, deploy sunshield, OTE, smss, antennae, )
- Cruise and Commissioning Phase (Align OTE, initialise and calibrate instruments, insert into L2 orbit, target Acquisition, )
- Normal Science Operations Phase (perform science operations, transmit data to ground for processing)
- Decommissioning Phase
